About TEDxBMU

A day of engaging talks, innovative ideas, and meaningful connections at Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology. TEDxBMU brings together thinkers, creators and doers to explore how diversity sparks innovation.

What is TEDx?

TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection.

TEDxBMU is independently organized under a license from TED, bringing the spirit of TED's mission of “ideas worth spreading” to Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology.

Our event features a combination of live speakers and TED Talk videos to spark deep conversation and connections. The TED Conference provides general guidance, but TEDxBMU is self-organized.
